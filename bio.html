Biography

rett Fischler was born on May 9, 1993. He grew up in a small town in Westchester, New York, and now resides in Wren Hall as a sophomore at Tufts University. After discovering his love for programming in a freshman year Visual Basic course, Brett has proceeded to take several more computer science classes, thereby fulfilling both his major requirements and his craving for new information. When Brett isn't programming, he can often be found playing tennis on the Tufts courts or teaching himself songs on his guitar.
